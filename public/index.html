Welcome to MySecureBucket.

Here, you can simply create a bucket with password and drop your files there. We promise that your password is NEVER transmitted over the wire. Your files are encrypted with your password in military security standard. You can also create a bucket without password to share your files.

Get Started

Create a Bucket

You can create a bucket with or without a password. But don't forget your password: we never have your password send to us, thus we cannot let you reset your password.
