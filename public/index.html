About MySecureBucket

MySecureBucket aims at letting people quickly and easily access files with highest security level. The passwords only lives within the browser and will never be transmtted over the Internet. All your files and metadata are encrypted using AES. You can share your files to your friends by just giving them the bucket name and password. We, the vender, or even NSA will never be able to access your files without your password.
